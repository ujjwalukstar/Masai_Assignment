3. Mental Health Awareness

Mental health awareness has gained significant momentum in recent years as people recognize the importance of emotional well-being. Efforts to destigmatize mental health issues have led to better access to resources and support systems. However, challenges persist, including cultural stigma, disparities in access to care, and the need for greater integration of mental health services into primary healthcare.
